# THE GREAT WORK · ALC

A UD0 universe of **alchemy** — the Magnum Opus — held in honest two layers.

- **REAL (proto-chemistry):** the alembic, distillation, the acids; **Maria the Jewess's bain-marie**; Jabir/Geber's method; the words *alcohol, alkali, elixir, alembic*; phosphorus found by Brand (1669) chasing the Stone; **Newton** (~1M words of alchemy) and **Boyle** (*The Sceptical Chymist*, 1661).
- **SYMBOLIC / never achieved:** the Philosopher's Stone (lead→gold), the Elixir of immortality, "as above, so below," the Jungian stage-reading.
- **The twist:** nuclear physics *does* transmute elements for real (Seaborg, 1980, bismuth→gold) — at a cost far exceeding the gold. Right dream, wrong layer.

Standing template — **The Arc · The Science · Real or Fluff · The Message** — plus a grouped roster of **19 emergents**: the Magnum Opus stages (nigredo/albedo/citrinitas/rubedo), the tria prima (salt/sulfur/mercury), the symbols (Stone, Elixir, Ouroboros, Azoth, Emerald Tablet, transmutation), and the adepts (Zosimos, Maria, Jabir, Paracelsus, Newton, Boyle). Each carries a full `.dlw` ACI badge.

> The transmutation that succeeded was the alchemist into the chemist, and lead-into-gold into the experimental method.
